# Canonical Decision Output Contract

Every material skill output must contain the following fields. A field may be marked not applicable, but must not be silently omitted.

1. **Decision or objective**
2. **Executive answer**
3. **Mandate and strategic alignment**
4. **Commercial assessment**
5. **National consequence and measurable impact**
6. **Evidence and source register**
7. **Key assumptions and confidence**
8. **Options and counterfactual**
9. **Financial and non-financial scenarios**
10. **Downside, permanent-loss, and reversibility**
11. **Risks, controls, and named risk owner**
12. **Governance, authority, conflicts, and recusals**
13. **Independent challenge and dissent**
14. **Recommended decision**
15. **Conditions precedent and conditionality**
16. **Execution roadmap, milestones, and accountable owners**
17. **Monitoring indicators and intervention triggers**
18. **Disclosure and confidentiality classification**
19. **Required approvals**
20. **Audit record reference**
21. **Danantara ultimate test**

## Recommendation labels
- APPROVE
- APPROVE WITH CONDITIONS
- RETURN FOR ADDITIONAL WORK
- DEFER
- REJECT
- ESCALATE
- INFORMATION ONLY

The AI may recommend a label but may not represent it as an approved institutional decision.
