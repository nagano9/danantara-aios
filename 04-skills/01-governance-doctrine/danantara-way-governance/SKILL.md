# Danantara Way Governance

## Objective
Translate Danantara's institutional DNA into repeatable decision controls so that mandate, capital, transformation, development impact, sovereignty, integrity, and long-term value are tested consistently.

## Use when
Use at the framing, analysis, challenge, approval, execution, monitoring, or post-decision stage of any material Danantara action.

## Do not use when
Do not use this doctrine to override law, formal policy, delegated authority, fiduciary duties, or competent corporate bodies. It is a governance kernel, not an independent source of legal power.

## Required inputs
Decision objective; mandate; proposal; commercial analysis; national-impact claim; risks; stakeholders; capital requirement; authority and approval path; confidentiality classification; time horizon.

## Authoritative sources
Use applicable law, official mandate, approved Danantara policy, delegations, committee charters, audited data, primary documents, independent evidence, and the controlled `DANANTARA_WAY.md`. Record any conflict between doctrine and formal authority.

## Workflow
1. Define the decision, counterfactual, affected capital, entity, stakeholders, and time horizon.
2. Select the relevant principles from the thirty-principle catalogue.
3. Apply the mandatory governance gates: authority, integrity, transparency, prudence, evidence, risk ownership, and audit trail.
4. Apply mission gates: DAM active ownership, DIM investment discipline, DDMF readiness and additionality, or BPI strategic alignment.
5. Quantify commercial return, national consequence, impact, resilience, capability, sovereignty, and opportunity cost separately.
6. Test all five core tensions and state which authority must resolve each unresolved trade-off.
7. Apply the ultimate test and issue a governance finding with conditions and escalation.

## Danantara Way decision rules
- **Purpose:** transform state assets into economic value, strategic capability, and intergenerational welfare.
- **Investment:** commercially disciplined, nationally consequential.
- **Asset management:** own actively, transform measurably, exit rationally.
- **Governance:** transparent, prudent, accountable, and auditable.
- **Technology:** build shared national capabilities, not fragmented applications.
- **Leadership:** understand the present, design the future, execute with measurable accountability.
- **Impact:** return, resilience, employment, capability, and sovereignty.
- **Ultimate test:** is the decision still right commercially, strategically, from a governance perspective, and for future generations?

## Output contract
Return:
- applicable principles and challenge questions
- commercial, strategic, governance, and intergenerational findings
- mandate and authority assessment
- five-tension register
- evidence, assumptions, and confidence
- risks, risk owners, conflicts, and disclosure classification
- recommendation, alternatives, conditions, approvals, and audit requirements

## Human approval
The competent authority decides how to resolve material trade-offs. AI may not treat doctrine as approval, alter formal mandate, or substitute a blended score for human judgment.

## Escalation
Escalate when commercial and national objectives cannot be reconciled transparently; direction lacks formal authority; support resembles bailout; confidentiality blocks necessary accountability; or the proposal fails integrity, prudence, or long-term-value tests.

## Prohibited actions
- Do not use “strategic” as a waiver of commercial discipline or governance.
- Do not use public impact as an unmeasured residual justification.
- Do not equate legal permissibility with integrity or prudence.
- Do not endorse merger, application procurement, foreign partnership, or capital support without quantified logic.
- Do not fabricate authority or approval.

## Quality checks
- All material principles were selected based on the actual decision, not a fixed checklist.
- Commercial and national findings are separately measurable.
- The five core tensions are explicit.
- The recommendation identifies conditions, owners, approvals, and consequences.
- Preferred vocabulary is used and restricted language is challenged.
- An independent reviewer can reproduce the conclusion.

## Audit record
Capture selected principles, questions asked, evidence, scores or qualitative findings, unresolved tensions, sponsor responses, independent challenge, approval authority, final human resolution, conditions, and subsequent outcomes.
