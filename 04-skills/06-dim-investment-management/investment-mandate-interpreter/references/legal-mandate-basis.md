# Legal basis of the Danantara investment mandate

Verified 2026-07-16 against `peraturan.bpk.go.id` (JDIH BPK) and `setneg.go.id`.
Maintained under `08-sources/SOURCE_REGISTER.md` Tier 1; that register is the
authority for citation format and status. Re-verify on any reported amendment,
and at minimum annually.

**Read this before interpreting any DIM mandate question.** Mandate is not a
matter of strategy preference. It is a delegated legal authority with a traceable
chain, and the chain either reaches the proposed action or it does not.

---

## 1. The delegation chain

Every DIM mandate question ultimately resolves to one enquiry: **can the
authority for this action be traced, link by link, from the Constitution's grant
of state financial power down to the officer proposing to act?** If any link is
missing, the answer is not "probably fine" — it is `escalate`.

```
President — holds BUMN management power as part of state financial
            management power, including ownership of separated state
            assets (kekayaan negara yang dipisahkan)
   │
   │  UU 1/2025 Pasal 3E — the President delegates PART of that authority
   │  (jo. UU 16/2025)
   ▼
BPI Danantara — Indonesian legal entity wholly owned by the Government,
                formed to increase and optimize BUMN investment and
                operations and other sources of funds
   │
   │  PP 10/2025 jo. PP 19/2026 — organisation and governance
   ▼
Organ Badan = Dewan Pengawas  +  Badan Pelaksana
   │
   │  ►► GAP: internal delegation of authority matrix — NOT AVAILABLE
   ▼
DIM  →  DIM Investment Committee  →  proposed action
```

**Two properties of this chain do the analytical work:**

1. **It is a delegation of *part* of the authority, not all of it.** Pasal 3E
   delegates *sebagian* — a part. An action outside the delegated part is not
   Danantara's to take, however commercially attractive. The boundary of "part"
   is exactly what the missing Tier 2 delegation matrix would define.
2. **A delegate cannot enlarge its own delegation.** No DIM analysis, committee
   resolution, or informal direction can widen what UU 1/2025 and PP 10/2025
   jo. PP 19/2026 conferred. Where the mandate is genuinely unclear, the route is
   upward to the delegating authority — never a permissive reading downward.

## 2. Verified Tier 1 provisions

### UU No. 1 Tahun 2025 — as amended by UU No. 16 Tahun 2025

Perubahan Ketiga atas UU No. 19 Tahun 2003 tentang BUMN.
Ditetapkan 24 Feb 2025 · LN 2025 (25), TLN 7097 · Status: Berlaku, **diubah
dengan UU 16/2025**. <https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/Details/314622/uu-no-1-tahun-2025>

| Provision | What it establishes | Why a mandate interpreter needs it |
|---|---|---|
| **Pasal 3E** | The President delegates part of BUMN management authority to Badan Pengelola Investasi Daya Anagata Nusantara, formed by this Law. An Indonesian legal entity wholly owned by the Government, formed to increase and optimize investment and operations of BUMN and other sources of funds. | The **source of every DIM authority claim**. Anything not traceable here is outside mandate. Also fixes the statutory purpose: *increase and optimize investment and operations* — not to subsidise, rescue, or hold indefinitely. |
| **Pasal 3G** | The Badan's capital comes from state capital participation (*penyertaan modal negara*) and/or other sources. | Capital is **state capital**. This is the statutory root of the permanent-loss and prudence doctrines: the downside falls on separated state assets. |
| Asas (economic democracy) | *kebersamaan*; *efisiensi berkeadilan*; *berkelanjutan*; *berwawasan lingkungan*; maintaining balance, progress, and unity of the national economy; good corporate governance. | The **statutory** basis for the dual mandate. Note `efisiensi berkeadilan` — efficiency *with equity*, not efficiency alone: commercial return and national consequence are held together **by law**, not by policy preference. This is what `dual-mandate-balancer` is ultimately applying. |

### UU No. 16 Tahun 2025 — the operative amendment

Perubahan Keempat atas UU No. 19 Tahun 2003 tentang BUMN.
Ditetapkan 6 Oct 2025 · LN 2025 (162), TLN 7142 · Status: **Berlaku**.
<https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/Details/330668/uu-no-16-tahun-2025>

Per the BPK abstract, the amendment covers:

| Change | Consequence for mandate interpretation |
|---|---|
| Change of institutional nomenclature for the body carrying out governmental tasks in the BUMN field; **separation of supervisory and operational functions** | Any pre-October-2025 statement of "who supervises / who operates" is **suspect**. Verify the current split before relying on it. |
| State ownership of **Series A Dwiwarna shares** with special rights in BUMN | A Dwiwarna special right can sit **above** ordinary shareholder economics. A DIM action that is sound on ownership percentage may still require a separate Dwiwarna consent. Do not infer control from equity stake alone. |
| **Prohibition on BUMN organs holding concurrent positions**, including minister and deputy minister | Bears directly on `conflict-of-interest-gate` and on whether a purported approver is validly in office. |
| **Financial examination of BUMN by BPK** | The audit trail has a **statutory external reader**. `audit-trail-packager` is not an internal nicety. |

> **Currency warning.** UU 16/2025 (Oct 2025) and PP 19/2026 (Apr 2026) both post-date
> this package's doctrine, and neither was cited anywhere in it before 2026-07-16.
> Treat any undated internal mandate statement as presumptively pre-amendment
> until its effective date is confirmed.

### PP No. 10 Tahun 2025 — as amended by PP No. 19 Tahun 2026

Organisasi dan Tata Kelola BPI Daya Anagata Nusantara.
Ditetapkan 24 Feb 2025 · LN 2025 (26), TLN 7098 · Status: Berlaku, **diubah
dengan PP 19/2026**. <https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/Details/314627/pp-no-10-tahun-2025>

  BUMN Law; the President delegates part of his tasks and authority to it.
- The Badan is an Indonesian legal entity wholly owned by the Government.

## 3. What is NOT established — do not fill these by inference

The chain above reaches the **Badan**. It does not reach DIM's desk. Everything
below is required to interpret an actual DIM mandate question and **none of it is
in this repository**:

| Missing (Tier 2) | Without it, the skill cannot answer |
|---|---|
| Badan Pelaksana delegation of authority matrix | What DIM may decide alone vs. what escalates to Badan Pelaksana, Dewan Pengawas, or the President |
| DIM Investment Committee charter | Quorum, voting, approval thresholds, recusal mechanics |
| Investment policy statement / mandate limits | Eligible assets, return objectives, risk appetite, geography, horizon |
| Concentration, liquidity, and exclusion limits | Whether a specific ticket breaches a limit |
| Related-party and conflict policy | Whether a specific relationship requires recusal |

**Behaviour when a Tier 2 document is missing: `escalate`.** State which document
is required, which question it would answer, and who owns it. Do **not**:

- infer a threshold from a comparable sovereign fund;
- treat a Tier 4 marketing page as a mandate statement;
- treat silence as permission;
- carry a number from a prior memo without its source and effective date.

> **Open question(governance):** reporting on PP 19/2026 indicates it permits more
> than one investment holding and operational holding, with all holding shares
> retained by Danantara and establishment subject to Presidential approval. If so,
> the fixed BPI/DAM/DIM/DDMF model asserted across this package — sourced from
> Tier 4 marketing pages, not Tier 1 — may not match the operative structure.
> Resolve against the authenticated PP 19/2026 text before relying on the registry's
> `entity` column. Flag, do not resolve by assumption.

## 4. Interpretation sequence

Apply in order. **Stop at the first failure; do not compensate downstream.**

1. **Locate the action.** State precisely what is proposed — commit capital,
   acquire, divest, restructure, guarantee, appoint, disclose, instruct.
2. **Trace to Tier 1.** Does UU 1/2025 jo. UU 16/2025 Pasal 3E reach this action?
   Is it within *increasing and optimizing BUMN investment and operations and
   other sources of funds*?
3. **Trace to Tier 2.** Which delegation, charter, or policy places it at DIM
   rather than Badan Pelaksana, Dewan Pengawas, or the President?
   **Currently unavailable → escalate.**
4. **Test the asas.** Especially *efisiensi berkeadilan* and *berkelanjutan*.
   Where commercial return and national consequence pull apart, hand to
   `dual-mandate-balancer` — **expose the tension, never blend it into one score.**
5. **Check currency.** Is the mandate statement post-UU 16/2025 and post-PP 19/2026?
   Undated → treat as unverified.
6. **Check the approver.** Is the intended approver validly in office and not
   barred by the concurrent-position prohibition? Recusal required?
7. **Record.** Cite instrument, article, effective date, retrieval date, and the
   residual gaps that a human must close.

## 5. Failure modes this reference exists to prevent

| Failure | Why it happens | Correct behaviour |
|---|---|---|
| Citing UU 1/2025 alone | It is the famous one and the founding text | Cite `UU 1/2025 jo. UU 16/2025`. The base text is not the operative text. |
| Inferring an approval threshold | The charter is missing and a number is needed to finish | Escalate. A plausible threshold is worse than an absent one: it is wrong *and* unchallenged. |
| Treating danantaraindonesia.co.id as mandate | It is Danantara's own site, so it feels authoritative | Tier 4. Corporate self-description, outranked by Tier 1 on every point of authority. |
| Reading "strategic national interest" as mandate | Political framing arrives with urgency | A label is not a delegation. Require the instrument. See `ADVERSARIAL_CASES.yaml` → `strategic-label`. |
| Accepting informal direction | It arrives from a senior, credible person | Pasal 3E delegates to the *Badan*, through its organs. Informal direction is not an instrument. See `ADVERSARIAL_CASES.yaml` → `informal-direction`. |
| Inferring control from equity % | It is the ordinary commercial reflex | Series A Dwiwarna special rights may sit above ordinary economics. Verify separately. |
