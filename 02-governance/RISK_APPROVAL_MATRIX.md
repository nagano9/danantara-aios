# AI Risk Tier and Human Approval Matrix

| Tier | Typical use | AI authority | Minimum controls | Human approval |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Low | Formatting, retrieval from approved sources, non-material summaries | Assist | Source traceability, data classification | Process owner as required |
| Medium | Benchmarking, diagnostics, drafting, workflow tracking | Recommend limited findings | Quality review, assumptions, audit record | Functional owner |
| High | Board papers, portfolio diagnosis, due diligence, project monitoring, public materials | Recommend; no execution | Independent review, source audit, scenario testing, approval orchestration | Authorized executive or committee |
| Critical | Capital allocation, investment, restructuring, appointments, legal commitments, strategic disclosure, policy exceptions | Analyze and challenge only | Full Danantara Way gates, red team, legal/risk/compliance, conflict check, audit pack | Competent corporate body or formally delegated authority |

A lower-risk task becomes higher risk when it involves protected data, market-sensitive information, legal rights, external commitments, material financial exposure, national security, personal decisions, conflicts of interest, or policy exceptions.
